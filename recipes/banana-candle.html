Banana Candle

The Candle Salad, invented in the 1920's will make you wonder what kind of party you just walked into!

This super simple "salad" from the 20's is enough to make your guests question the intentions of your invitation!

Ingredients

  • 1 crisp lettuce leaf
  • 1 slice of pineapple
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 pineapple slice
  • 1 maraschino cherry
  • 1 toothpick

Instructions

  1. Place crisp lettuce leaf on a plate
  2. For candle base place the slice of pineapple down on the lettuce
  3. Place the 1/2 banana upright in the center of the pineapple slice
  4. Fasten the maraschino cherry to the tip of the banana using the toothpick

And there you have it! Better than a real candle, because you can eat it! Up to you to figure out how I suppose!
